Recruitment Information

Apply to HERO!

Applications for our Fall 2024 Recruitment Cycle are now open!

Freshmen (class of 2028) and Sophomores (class of 2027) are eligible to apply. Licensed EMTs in any state may apply through our “priors” process, and applicants looking to get their license through HERO may apply through the “non-priors” process. Applications close on September 29th at 11:59pm, and no exceptions wil be made past this deadline. Thank you for your interest in our unit, and we look forward to reading your applications!

Priors: Fall 2024 HERO Current EMT Application apply here!

Non-priors: Fall 2024 HERO New EMT Application apply here!
