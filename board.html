Leadership Team

Meet the administrative and operational heart of HERO

Overview

HERO has two branches: an administrative branch overseen by the Board of Directors and an operational branch overseen by the Operations Board. Together, the boards work to maintain the integrity of HERO's policy decisions, mission, and vision.

Meet the Board


Captain: Tom Wang Email

Tom Wang is honored to be serving as the Captain for 2024. Wang majors in Public Health and Behavioral Biology (co. 2026). Captain Wang started his EMS journey his junior year of High School after seeing a sign at his local volunteer fire department. He earned his NREMT and Maryland EMT-B license during his senior year of high school and has been working to serve his community ever since.
