Board of Directors

Meet the administrative and operational heart of HERO

Overview

The HERO Board of Directors is comprised of the three operational officers, the captain, personnel officer, and equipment officer, and four administrative officers. Together, the Board of Directors works to maintain the integrity of HERO's policy decisions, mission, and vision.

Meet the Board


Captain Samantha Camp Email

Samantha is a junior biophysics major from Glenmoore, Pennsylvania. She has volunteered as both a firefighter and EMT at her hometown fire company for 5 years. Samantha has previously served as the unit's Training Officer. Outside of HERO, Samantha does neurosurgery research at Johns Hopkins Hospital, theoretical biophysics research, and hangs out with members of her sorority, Pi Beta Phi.












Personnel Officer Anthony Garcia Email

Anthony is a junior Molecular & Cellular Biology and Public Health Major from Liberty, Missouri. He joined HERO his freshman year as a new EMT, and has previously served as Recruitment and Retention Officer. Outside of HERO, Anthony does biochemistry research at the Bloomberg School of Public Health and is the director of the Johns Hopkins Outdoors Club where he leads whitewater and caving trips.
